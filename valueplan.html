30 / 60 / 90 / 120 Plan

High-level plan to accelerate value in a Senior QA Lead role. This page provides a recruiter-friendly outline of priorities and deliverables for early months on the job.

First 30 days

  • Understand product scope, release cadence, and key stakeholders
  • Review existing test artifacts, environments, and defect workflows
  • Establish communication cadence for QA status and triage

30–60 days

  • Refine test plans and acceptance criteria with BA and product teams
  • Implement traceability between requirements and test coverage
  • Identify quick wins to reduce release risk

60–90 days

  • Stabilize regression and UAT processes and reporting
  • Coordinate cross-team SIT and integration validation
  • Deliver measurable improvements to defect turnaround and release readiness

90–120 days

  • Embed QA metrics into release decision-making
  • Document repeatable QA processes and handoffs
  • Plan for longer-term automation and monitoring improvements
