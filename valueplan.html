30 / 60 / 90 / 120 Day Plan

How I would approach value delivery in a Senior QA Lead role.

High-level plan focused on learning the environment, clarifying scope, strengthening traceability, improving test execution discipline, and supporting release readiness.

First 30 days

  • Understand products, scope, stakeholders, and release cadence
  • Review environments, test artifacts, and defect processes
  • Assess current QA communication and escalation flow
60

30–60 days

  • Refine test planning and traceability across priority areas
  • Strengthen alignment with business, product, and development teams

90

60–90 days

  • Improve regression, SIT, and UAT execution consistency
  • Support clearer defect triage and risk reporting
  • Increase readiness confidence for upcoming releases
120

90–120 days

  • Embed repeatable QA processes and dashboards
  • Improve requirement-to-test visibility across teams
  • Plan longer-term improvements for automation and monitoring
