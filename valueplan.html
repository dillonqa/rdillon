Value plan

30 / 60 / 90 / 120-day view of early contribution.

High-level plan focused on understanding the environment, clarifying requirements, improving traceability, strengthening execution discipline, increasing visibility, and supporting confident release decisions.

30

First 30 Days

Listen • Learn • Map

Foundation and Discovery

  • Learn the products, workflows, stakeholders, environments, and release cadence.
  • Review current QA processes, test assets, evidence quality, and defect flow.
  • Understand requirements coverage, traceability, and testing priorities.

60

30 to 60 Days

Prioritize • Align • Improve

Strategic Alignment and Early Wins

  • Strengthen alignment across business, product, development, and QA teams.
  • Improve test planning, traceability, and visibility across priority work.
  • Identify gaps in test coverage, defect communication, and release-readiness support.

90

60 to 90 Days

Execute • Stabilize • Support

Execution in Motion

  • Support stronger SIT, UAT, integration testing, and validation discipline.
  • Improve defect triage, evidence quality, reporting clarity, and risk visibility.
  • Use practical tools, including AI support where helpful, to strengthen test design and issue review.

120

90 to 120 Days

Scale • Sustain • Lead

Repeatable Value

  • Support repeatable QA processes, dashboards, and reporting for stronger delivery visibility.
  • Strengthen requirements-to-test traceability and release-readiness discipline.
  • Help define longer-term improvements across testing, issue follow-through, and team coordination.
