Senior QA Lead value plan

Plan phases

First 30 days

  • Understand product scope, release cadence, and stakeholders.
  • Review test artifacts, environments, and defect workflows.
  • Establish QA communication and triage cadence.

30–60 days

  • Refine test plans and acceptance criteria with BA and product teams.
  • Implement traceability between requirements and tests.
  • Identify quick wins to reduce release risk.

60–90 days

  • Stabilize regression and UAT processes and reporting.
  • Coordinate cross‑team SIT and integration validation.
  • Improve defect turnaround and release readiness metrics.

90–120 days

  • Embed QA metrics into release decision‑making.
  • Document repeatable QA processes and handoffs.
  • Plan longer‑term automation and monitoring improvements.
