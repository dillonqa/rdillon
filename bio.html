Executive summary

Raquel Dillon is a Senior QA professional with experience supporting enterprise platforms across automotive, government, hospitality, education, financial services, and digital media. Her background combines QA leadership with business analysis support, which allows her to work across both validation execution and the business context behind it.

Throughout her career, she has worked closely with business, product, and development teams to review requirements, clarify workflows, support acceptance criteria, maintain requirements traceability, and align testing to business needs. Her work is grounded in practical delivery, not just documentation. That includes test planning, SIT, UAT, integration testing, API testing, ETL testing, SQL validation, data reconciliation, defect management, and release readiness support.

At General Motors, she supported dealer application rollout efforts by onboarding dealers and vendors, reviewing requirements to confirm scope and testability, and leading UAT, integration, and API testing across dealer and B2B vendor platforms. Her work also included backend SQL and SAP HANA validation, ETL-related testing, and issue investigation using logs, monitoring tools, and evidence capture.

At Civix, she supported large-scale SaaS platforms used by government agencies, where she served as Scrum QA Lead and helped teams move work forward through regression tracking, requirements review, accessibility testing, traceability support, QA dashboards, and post-deployment validation.

Her Walt Disney World experience spans both a Senior QA / BA Lead contract role and a long-term Senior Tech Specialist role. Across those roles, she supported pricing and analytics validation, ETL testing, reporting checks, SQL-based data review, requirements coverage analysis, and enterprise QA delivery for systems supporting parks, hotels, transportation, cruise, finance, HR, and resort operations.

In education and financial services, she led UAT coordination, ERP modernization support, SIT, workflow validation, reconciliation checks, accessibility checks, and defect lifecycle management using tools such as Jira, Azure DevOps, Postman, SoapUI, BrowserStack, HP ALM/Quality Center, and SQL-based validation environments.

What makes her background effective in QA Lead, Senior QA, UAT, SIT, and Integration Testing roles is her ability to connect requirements, validation, evidence, and release decisions.

She is best positioned in teams that need strong QA leadership, hands-on validation depth, clear requirements support, traceability discipline, and reliable release readiness coordination.
